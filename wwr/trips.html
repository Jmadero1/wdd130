Trips

Embark on an unforgettable journey with River Adventure Rafting! Our thrilling rafting trips offer the perfect blend of adrenaline-pumping excitement and breathtaking natural beauty. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-time adventurer, our expert guides will ensure a safe and exhilarating experience for all.

Ready to book your adventure? Browse our selection of trips below and reserve your spot today!

View Trips!

1/2 Day

This thrilling half-day excursion offers the perfect opportunity to enjoy the adrenaline of rafting without committing too much time. For approximately half a day, you'll venture through exciting rapids and beautiful river landscapes. Perfect for those with limited time but still wanting to experience the thrill of rafting.

1 Day

Get ready for an action-packed adventure with our thrilling full-day rafting trip! You'll spend the entire day navigating through exciting rapids while marveling at the stunning natural beauty surrounding you. In addition to rafting, you'll enjoy a delicious outdoor lunch by the river, provided as part of your experience.

2 Days

Immerse yourself in an exciting two-day rafting expedition where you'll have the chance to further explore the river and its surroundings. During this trip, you'll camp under the stars on the riverbanks and share stories around the campfire. Experience nature at its finest as you embark on this multi-day rafting adventure.

3 Days

Join us for a once-in-a-lifetime experience with our three-day wilderness rafting trip. This journey will take you through thrilling rapids while immersing you in the wild beauty of the surrounding nature. In addition to rafting, you'll have the opportunity to explore canyons, waterfalls, and natural trails during your days on the river. You'll camp in remote natural settings, surrounded by tranquility and beauty.

4 or 5 Days

Embark on the ultimate rafting adventure with our four to five-day trip. This epic experience will take you through multiple days of thrilling rapids, spectacular landscapes, and a true connection with nature. With overnight stays in remote campsites and outdoor meals under the stars, this expedition will provide you with memories for a lifetime as you explore the best that nature has to offer.
